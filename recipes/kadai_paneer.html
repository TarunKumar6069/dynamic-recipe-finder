Kadai Paneer

This is a delicious recipe made with paneer in kadai

Kadai Paneer is a popular North Indian dish made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese), bell peppers, onions, and a freshly ground spice mix known as kadai masala. The dish gets its name from the traditional Indian wok called a kadai in which it is cooked.

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Capsicum
  • Blend of spices like coriander seeds, red chillies, cumin, and garam masala

Steps

  1. Dry roast and grind coriander seeds & red chilies.
  2. Sauté onions in oil till golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook till soft.
  5. Mix in spices and kadai masala.
  6. Add capsicum and cook briefly.
  7. Add paneer cubes and stir gently.
  8. Sprinkle garam masala & kasuri methi.
  9. Simmer for 2–3 minutes and serve hot.
