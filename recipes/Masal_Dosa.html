Masala Dosa

This is a tasty and popular south indian dish made with rice and vegetables

Dosa is a thin, crispy South Indian pancake made from fermented rice and urad dal batter. It is typically served hot with coconut chutney and sambar. Loved for its light texture and savory flavor, it's a popular breakfast and street food.

Ingredients

  • Parboiled Rice
  • Urad Dal(black lentils)
  • Fenugreek seeds
  • Potato Filling

Steps

  1. Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds for 6 hours.
  2. Grind to a smooth batter and ferment overnight.
  3. Prepare potato filling with boiled potatoes, onions, mustard seeds, and spices.
  4. Heat tawa and pour a ladle of batter in the center.
  5. Spread into a thin circle and drizzle oil around edges.
  6. Cook until crisp and place potato filling in the center.
  7. Fold dosa over filling and serve hot with chutney and sambar.
