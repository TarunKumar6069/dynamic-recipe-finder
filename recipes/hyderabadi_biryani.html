Hyderabadi Biryani

This is most popular dish in India made with basmati rice and meat

Hyderabadi Biryani is a world-famous dish from the royal kitchens of Hyderabad, known for its rich aroma, deep flavors, and regal presentation. It's a layered rice dish made by cooking spiced marinated chicken and fragrant basmati rice together using the traditional dum (steam) method. This biryani stands out for its use of saffron, fried onions, mint, and a special blend of spices that infuse every grain of rice with flavor. Often served with raita or salan, Hyderabadi Biryani is a celebratory meal that brings both heritage and taste to the table.

Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice
  • Meat(mutton or chicken)
  • Yogurt
  • Fried onions
  • Ghee
  • Spices like saffron, ginger-garlic paste, and garam masala

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, and herbs.
  2. Soak basmati rice and partially cook it with whole spices.
  3. Layer marinated chicken at the bottom of a heavy pot.
  4. Add a layer of half-cooked rice on top.
  5. Sprinkle fried onions, saffron milk, mint, and ghee.
  6. Cover the pot tightly (use dough or a lid).

  8. Let it rest, then gently mix and serve hot.
